About Arcturus

Where ideas take flight

The Arcturus MCS (Modular Cubesat System) is designed for small-scale research teams, startups, and academic institutions, offering unparalleled flexibility and capability for deploying scientific payloads and conducting space research.

Arcturus SSS Program

Satellite Sharing Service

For institutions with little need of module (less than 3) and limited budget, Arcturus SSS Program allows at max three customers sharing one MCS, each has complete ownage of Module and shares the same Core Unit. Each customer will only need to pay for the induvisually owned Module(s), a discounted service fee (up to 10% off) and shared Core Unit and orital launch price
